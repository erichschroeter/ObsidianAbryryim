Touched by a sliver of divine power, the Knights of Prophecy are granted insight into the ascension prophecy of one deity for the next Cycle. The Knights of Prophecy are drawn to the Island of Prophecy to submit the details of the prophecy to the Council. Thereafter, the Knight is empowered to protect the prophecy from those that would subdue it. This subclass can be adopted by any class that is granted a subclass at level 3.  
3rd level: If you have the Spellcasting class feature, the following spells are added to the available options for spells known, and are automatically prepared not counting against the limit for number of prepared spells:  
Level Spells 3rd protection from evil and good, detect evil and good 5th detect thoughts, zone of truth 9th beacon of hope, dispel magic 13th freedom of movement, divination 17th commune, legend lore  
Divine Tracker: Knights of Prophecy are skilled at tracking down enemies of the faith, and those that would seek to subvert the prophecy they have been enlightened to. You gain proficiency in either the Investigation or Survival skill and may add double your proficiency bonus to the selected skill.  
Prophetic Vision: The sliver of divine blessing provided to the Knights of Prophecy can be channeled to protect the Knight or aid in vanquishing enemies. This power is represented by 4d6 Vision Dice that refresh on a long rest. Vision Dice may used in the following ways, but no more than one die can be used at a time: Add +1d6 to an attack roll, saving throw, or skill check after the roll is made, but before the DM confirms success or failure As a Reaction, you may subtract 1d6 from an attack roll made by a creature that you can see Spend two Vision Dice and touch a creature to cure the target of one disease or neutralize one poison affecting it  
7th Level - Truth Sets You Free: Once per long rest you can emit a Zone of Truth spell centered on yourself for up to 10 minutes. You gain advantage on all Insight checks made against creatures inside the radius.  
11th Level- Vision Enhancement Once you reach 11th level, your Vision Dice increase to 1d8s  
15th - Purity of Spirit: At 15th level the Knight of Prophecy stops aging and is immune to effects that cause aging. In addition, attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws made by the Knight of Prophecy cannot be affected by abilities, spells or effects from allies or enemies that add advantage or disadvantage to the roll. This does not apply to any Conditions the Knight may be suffering from (i.e. Poisoned condition still applies disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws).
