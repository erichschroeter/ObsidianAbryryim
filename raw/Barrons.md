## BARONY OF SILVERBROOK

The Barony of Silverbrook guards the crossroads of rivers and roads that link north and south. Granted to Lord Edrin Silverbrook for his wisdom in negotiation and his skill in uniting rival settlements, the barony became a center of trade and diplomacy. The bridge signifies connection and safe passage, while the star represents guidance and hope.

## BARONY OF OAKHOLLOW

The Barony of Oakhollow lies within ancient woodlands that have sheltered generations. Founded by Lady Mara Oakheart, a protector of the old ways and the natural world, the barony is known for its steadfast independence and deep respect for tradition. The oak signifies strength and endurance, while the acorns symbolize growth and renewal.

## BARONY OF STONEFELL

The Barony of Stonefell holds the mountain passes that defend the realm from northern threats. It was granted to Lord Garric Stonefist, a warrior whose courage never faltered. The mountains represent endurance and unyielding defense, while the tower stands for vigilance and the unbreakable watch.
