	##### The Beginning
	**Founding**
	**The First Age began when an incredibly powerful being created the world of Abryryim, and linked it to the known planes of existence, tying it to the multiverse. They had seen that other lesser gods had become too obsessed with their divinity and disliked that they failed to maintain the worlds they should have played an active role governing. Out of this frustration Abryryim was born. This being implemented an insurmountable rule that the deities of this new world may only possess divinity for 1,000 years before they're forced to return to mortality. A new purpose is gained through taking on flesh and blood: to ensure fulfillment of the Prophecies that each new Age demands. The being that fulfills a prophecy then ascends to godhood for the next Age, with new prophecies to be fulfilled so the Cycle of Ascension continues.** **The Ages are as follows: Dawn, Magic, Steel, Nature, Lava, Waxing, Waning, Light, Darkness, and Dusk. So, time would be set up like this, 5369 year is called Age of Lava 369 or 12984 year would be 2nd Age of Magic 984.**
