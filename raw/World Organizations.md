# New organizations:
**Order of the Reflecting Pool:** The individuals are the seekers or God artifacts and other items that could be used to cause mass death within the world. They do not follow any certain god but all the gods in the name of not allowing major events to pass.
**Horsemen of Phitare:** were a group whose membership consisted of ex-prisoners and criminals that had banded together. They raid anything, using the goods and money to push cities under their control. They have people in almost every city.
**Sect of the Divine Reach:** This Sect believe it is the divine hand of the gods, past gods and the newest gods. They will hunt out anyone who opposes what they believe is truth about the gods. Zealots at best fanatics mostly.
**Stonepuppets Maison’s:** The secret underground (both physically and metaphorically) group of builders that are used to build only the temples of the gods. These builders have been around since the first age. The knowledge of the temples in the world is unending. They protect the building’s true plan and all its secrets.
**Liars Syndicate:** The largest group of the Secret Organization, the men and women of this group are hired killers. They kill only for the money; they do not care if you are good or evil. However, there code is they will never kill children under the age of 12. Instead, if orphaned and under 12 they will take them under their protection and train the next generation of Liars Syndicate.
**The Seekers of the Lost:** This organization looks to find lost temples, lost cities and other items lost in time. They can act as guides for groups or teachers within the cities. You can find this organization in most cities paying as a guide.
**Hermetic Order Brightest Light:** This Order is a very quiet order; they only engage in the world during the Cleansing. The year before the Cleansing the order goes out to the cities to start proclaiming the coming of the new age. They also help to stabilize cities and the land as the world goes through the Cleansing.
**Fraction Cross Merchants:** The merchet guild is one of the few that are willing to travel across the lava fields and sulfur fields. Due to this being a very dangerous job the people who protect the caverns are paid very well. The good that travel between the two continents are run by someone from the organization either by land or sea.
**Order of the 13:** This order follows very powerful devils. They perform rituals to enhance their own power, gain wealth, or other things that may wish for. The order is hidden from most people in the cities. They are wanting the world to come to an end and work towards that end.
**Rehtaflla:** Perhaps one of the most powerful secret societies in the world is the Rehtaflla. It emerged early in the 2nd Age and claimed to show people a secret esoteric order from which they could learn from and follow. In doing so, new Rehtaflla could gain invaluable wisdom into nature, the physical world, and the spiritual world. It is believed that the Rehtaflla use occult magic to engender not only a spiritual transformation but a global transformation.
**Jinog-Tis:** were regular farmers by day, but at night, they were fearful killers. They were notorious for their fearless attitudes, not afraid to cut down their targets despite the security detail that surrounded them. Over the course of three ages the Jinog-Tis executed viziers, sultans, leaders of the Chruches, and more. Between 2nd and the 3rd age more than eight Grand Masters ruled over the group. But the Jinog-Tis would lose much of their power at the hands of the Liars Syndicate, who essentially destroyed the group during the 3rd age Cleansing, forcing the killers to disperse into the darkness.They active seek revenge to hunt the Syndicate
