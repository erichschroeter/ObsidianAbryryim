I’ve worked as a scribe for most of my life, copying books, scrolls, letters, and whatever else people needed written down. I moved between different scriptoria over the years and spent a lot of time around magical texts. After seeing the same spells over and over, I got curious and started copying a few into my own spellbook and practicing them when I had free time.

I never really went to a proper magic school. Most of what I learned came from working as a scribe and asking questions whenever someone was willing to answer them. For many years I worked at a magical college where my mother, Lady Aurelix Sunspire, teaches magic, so I was lucky enough to spend my days surrounded by books and people who knew far more than I did.

I’ve been doing this for a long time, and along the way I met all kinds of scholars, librarians, and travelers. I never thought those old acquaintances would matter much, but a few of them ended up connected to the party and the funeral that started our story
