# Iepos

Iepos is a unique island city located within a temporal vortex, where inhabitants do not age.

## Description
Home to 2,900 souls, Iepos exists in a time distortion. Those invited are unaffected until they leave; if they return, they age one year for every minute spent there. The city has no ruler; every person is a teacher. It is the source of the prophecies that are delivered to the mainland by the [[Knights of Prophecy]].

## Interesting Facts
- Even the undead [[Forbarrens]] will age to dust if they enter this area.
- Prophecies are taken from here to the mainland.
