# Lakobos

Lakobos is a fishing village built partially over the water, specializing in the raising of great eels.

## Description
Home to 9,000 souls, Lakobos is ruled by Lord Snith-del-fonta, a Yaun-ti. The city is famous for its large pens of great eels (up to 20 feet long), which are used for food and hides. Armor made from Lakobos eel hide is highly flexible. The city ubiquitously worships Thym'jiir, the Twice Drowned God.

## Interesting Facts
- Contains underwater homes for sea elves and other aquatic races.
- The 3rd leg of the [[Four-Fold Endurance]] starts here.
- Thym'jiir lived here before achieving divinity.
