# Cillicia

Cillicia is a major agricultural hub and neutral state known for its vast herds and trade ports on Thrumos Bay.

## Description
Cillicia is home to 22,000 souls and a massive number of livestock. It is a neutral state open to free trade, consisting of many loose-fitting villages. It is governed by a collective of 59 representatives re-elected every three years. The political center is on Thrumos Bay. Some older sections are built into 1000-foot cliffs, using pulley systems to transport goods to the docks.

## Interesting Facts
- The climate creates irregular tornadoes and windstorms; locals say "never trust the wind when the clouds talk."
- Cillicia has remained neutral in nearly all wars and conflicts on Quatois.
- The Zephyr Mountains influence its near-perfect grazing climate.
