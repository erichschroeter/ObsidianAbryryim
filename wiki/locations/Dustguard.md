# Dustguard

Dustguard is the home of the Knights of the Sand and is known for its rigorous trials for young men.

## Description
Dustguard is home to 13,000 souls. Boys at age 10 undergo the "Trial of Tears." Those who pass choose between the Path of Leadership (joining the Council of Dust, cannot marry) or the Path of Protection (city guard, encouraged to have children). Those who fail or do not take the test become workers.

## Interesting Facts
- Each year the city floods the surrounding area to slow attacks from Ragemaw, using special bridges for travelers.
- The flooded fields are used to grow rice and other plants.
