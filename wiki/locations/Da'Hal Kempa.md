# Da'Hal Kempa

Da'Hal Kempa is the largest city in its area, primarily inhabited by elves and known for its defensible position and ancient libraries.

## Description
Da'Hal Kempa is home to 40,000 souls, mostly elves. Its frigid cold and defensible position have kept it safe for centuries. It houses the oldest library in the world, the "Santum Fe Na Deso" (Home of our Past). Major industries include carpentry, crystalline weapon production, music, art, and the production of Gaza wine from ancient onranos trees.

## Interesting Facts
- Gaza wine is made from berries harvested every 25 years.
- A single Gaza tree can be sold for 30,000 gp.
- Four major elven houses control the Gaza wine trade: Tayos, Xiovi, Evipo, and Aepio.
- Some families have lived through the last [[The Cleansing]].
