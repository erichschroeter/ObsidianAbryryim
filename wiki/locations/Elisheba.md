# Elisheba

Elisheba is the oldest city in Abryryim, home to 85,000 souls and defined by its strict adherence to the number eight.

## Description
Elisheba is believed to be the origin of all life on Abryryim. The city is ruled by the [[Council of Eight]] and is strictly divided into eight sectors, districts, blocks, and buildings. It is a vertical city with three current tiers (eventually expanding to eight) that represent extreme economic and social disparity.

## Interesting Facts
- Tier 3 (the rich) live above the clouds and rarely touch the earth.
- Tier 2 consists of laborers and Tier 1 of the dregs of society and animal handlers.
- During the First Age [[The Cleansing]], the city defeated an army of 10,000; the enemy's weapons are now mounted on the walls and used for execution by slow impalement.
- The city is near the Ancient's Pool, home to hags, lizardfolk, and other mystical creatures.
