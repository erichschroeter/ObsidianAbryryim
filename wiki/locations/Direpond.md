# Direpond

Direpond is the last major city before the Dreadlands, primarily a dwarven settlement focused on mining and providing clean water.

## Description
Home to 12,000 souls, mostly dwarves, Direpond is a vital stop for gathering clean water from deep aqueducts before entering the Dreadlands. The city features hard clay buildings with thatching that must be replaced frequently due to sulfuric rains. Thousands of residents live and work year-round in underground mining tunnels.

## Interesting Facts
- Home to four major dwarven clans, each owning a specific mining operation: silver, iron, crimson ore, and ebon ore.
- Clansmen are tattooed at birth; unauthorized mining can lead to the loss of an ear or banishment.
- Sulfuric rains in winter can corrode metal within three weeks, though crimson ore is unaffected.
