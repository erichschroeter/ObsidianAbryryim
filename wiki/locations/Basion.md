# Basion

Basion, also known as the City of Beasts or City of Animals, is a major hub for hunters and the trade of furs and hides from migrating animals.

## Description
Basion is home to 16,000 souls and is run by a city council of elder hunters who hold their positions for life. The city is situated near major water sources where over 4 million animals (lions, gazelles, elephants, wildebeests) migrate within a 200-mile radius. The city's economy thrives on the sale of furs and hides across the land.

## Interesting Facts
- Over 12,000 animals are killed annually on average.
- During migration, dragons can be seen hunting in the area.
- Many exotic animals are sold in the city for high prices.
