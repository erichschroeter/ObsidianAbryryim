# Katoteros Desert

The Katoteros Desert is a vast, cursed wasteland that was once lush farmland during the Age of Dawn.

## Description
During the 2nd Age, powerful spellcasters attempted to build a massive portal called the [[Aditus]]. The experiment went catastrophically wrong, causing a plague that melted the wizards into their towers, which then exploded. These explosions sent geysers of sand into the skies, which slowly covered the land over 1000 years. The geysers only stopped when the crater [[Putes Aminus]] was created in Thrumos Bay.
