# Thrumos

Thrumos is a city known for its strict prohibition of arcane magic and its proximity to the mysterious crater [[Putes Aminus]].

## Description
Home to 14,000 souls, Thrumos is ruled by General Korell Nugam. The General forbids arcane magic, which some believe is due to the crater [[Putes Aminus]] in Thrumos Bay. Despite the strict military rule, the city is prosperous, and no one goes hungry thanks to a seaweed-based food ration system.

## Interesting Facts
- The crater [[Putes Aminus]] was created by a shard of the moon Welun and is rumored to be a portal.
- General Korell Nugam is whispered to be a secret Necromancer.
- The city is a melting pot of diverse races, including monstrous humanoids.
