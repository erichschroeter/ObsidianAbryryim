# East Lake

East Lake is a hardy town located on a lake, serving as a staging point for adventurers traveling into the [[Pillars of Heaven]].

## Description
Home to 12,000 souls, East Lake provides a food source and land for crops. Due to its proximity to the [[Pillars of Heaven]], the town must defend against gnoll raids and desert monsters. It is famous for being a starting point for those seeking diamonds in the nearby plateaus.

## Interesting Facts
- Large millipedes are sometimes used to move goods.
- The largest diamond found in the area was the size of a human fist.
