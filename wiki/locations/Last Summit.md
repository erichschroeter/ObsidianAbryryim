# Last Summit

Last Summit is a staging city for crusaders traveling into the [[Pillars of Heaven]] to retrieve holy relics.

## Description
Home to 18,000 souls, Last Summit is ruled by the dictator Lord Knight Atticus Acisculus. The city's purpose is to support those seeking relics of dead gods in the [[Pillars of Heaven]]. Notable relics found include the Broken Crown of Kaikl and the Caxn Shadow Blade.

## Interesting Facts
- Artifacts are often encased within the pillars and require scaling hundreds of them to find.
- Deadly fumes from the area can kill within an hour if the wind blows in the wrong direction.
