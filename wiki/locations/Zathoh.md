# Zathoh

Zathoh is a city built within the trees of the Parinos Woods, home to "tree folk" and ancient living trees.

## Description
Home to 10,000 souls, Zathoh was originally built by elves and aarakocra to escape forest predators. A plague in the 2nd Age nearly wiped out the aarakocra, and many elves left. The city is now a diverse community living in the high canopy.

## Interesting Facts
- Home to the "High Trees," 12 ancient trees believed to be among the oldest living things on the planet.
- "Sorrow Moss" is harvested from nearby bluffs and used to make long-lasting bread for armies.
- The city is built in an old-growth forest.
