# Bellfield

Bellfield is a sister city to [[Clayfield]] and is primarily inhabited by [[Avitum]] who adhere to the traditional 'Way of the Leaf.'

## Description
Home to 7,000 souls, most of whom are [[Avitum]], Bellfield is run as a collective with no single ruler. This decision-making process is slow, often taking months to update rules. The city produces over 80% of the food consumed in its region of Abryryim. The city consists of simple structures spread out over 2 miles in a random, forest-like arrangement.

## Interesting Facts
- Mostly inhabited by [[Avitum]] males.
- The inhabitants enjoy working in the sun.
