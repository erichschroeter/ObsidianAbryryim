# Arachine Swamp

The Arachine Swamp is one of the least traveled areas in Abryryim, known for its unnatural and inhospitable weather and persistent mist.

## Description
Weather in the Arachine Swamp is a place of unending discomfort. Rain falls regularly, regardless of the season. The annual precipitation is approximately 70 inches. Temperatures range from the 90s in summer/autumn to the 40s in winter/spring. It never snows. The marsh and forest are forever shrouded with a persistent, thin, and sticky mist that moves almost like a living thing, obscuring visibility beyond a mile.
