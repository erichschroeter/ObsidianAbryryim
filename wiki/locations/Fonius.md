# Fonius

Fonius is a collection of tribes inhabited by swamp runners and trappers, known for its dangerous environment and potent moonshine.

## Description
Home to 8,000 souls, mostly wood elves, kobolds, goblins, and kenku, Fonius lacks a formal ruler. The economy is based on alligator and snake hides, as well as rare mushrooms used for poisons and "Yellow Death" moonshine.

## Interesting Facts
- The locals are known to trick outsiders into drinking contests to steal their belongings.
- The 2nd Leg of the [[Four-Fold Endurance]] starts here.
