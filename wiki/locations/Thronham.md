# Thronham

Thronham is a thriving port city on the Paragon River, serving as a major hub for global trade.

## Description
Home to about 20,000 souls, Thronham is the main port city in the land. It is ruled by Od'arr Brightshield, a half-orc Paladin, and his "Sunsword" city guard. The city is famous for its falconry industry and annual tournaments.

## Interesting Facts
- The city features a network of winding canals and "floating markets."
- Pupparins, gondolas, and topettas are common modes of transportation.
- Criminals are treated harshly with public ridicule and branding.
