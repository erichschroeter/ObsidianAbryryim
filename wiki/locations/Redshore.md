# Redshore

Redshore is a prosperous port town famous for its trade with giants and its wealthy jewelry industry.

## Description
Home to 10,000 souls, Redshore is the only mainland city that trades with the giants of western Yenos. The trade was established by the bard Hawthorne, who traded steel for giant gems. The city is extremely wealthy, featuring beachside manors and luxurious lifestyles.

## Interesting Facts
- The "Eye of Hawthorne," a massive enchanted sapphire, purifies the town's iron-laden water.
- "Cleansing Girdles," enchanted gem fragments, are common items used for cleaning.
- The city is guarded by mercenaries from [[Knights Drift]].
