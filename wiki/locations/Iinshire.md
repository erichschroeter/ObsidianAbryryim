# Iinshire

Iinshire is a city that guards a key pass through the Zephyr Mountains, serving as a hub for trade and mercenary work.

## Description
Home to 12,000 souls, Iinshire is ruled by the drow elf Lord Actics Von Delarose. The city thrives on guarding caravans and mercenary contracts. It is also the host city for the [[Four-Fold Endurance]] competition, which brings significant profit to the city.

## Interesting Facts
- Clay from the nearby Hills of Chilki is used to make dense, sturdy bricks.
- The city profits massively from the 500+ teams that join the [[Four-Fold Endurance]] annually.
