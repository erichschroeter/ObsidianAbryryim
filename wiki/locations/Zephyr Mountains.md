# Zephyr Mountains

The Zephyr Mountains are among the highest in the world, dividing the continent into three areas.

## Description
The mountains are home to several underground kingdoms that have never seen the light of day. They are extremely difficult to traverse, and only the hardiest travelers attempt to cross them.
