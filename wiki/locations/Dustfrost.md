# Dustfrost

Originally a dwarven stronghold, Dustfrost is now a militant city inhabited by Dragonborn.

## Description
Dustfrost is home to 8,000 souls. It was built in the first age by dwarves but later abandoned. A clan of Dragonborn from the Arachine Swamp discovered and claimed it. The city is highly militant and follows three orders: The Petikos (nobles), The Ntos (commonfolk), and The Aette sect (judges).

## Interesting Facts
- The Dragonborn follow a strict code: harm nothing of the city-state, keep noble bloodlines pure, and venerate Giten.
- The city was originally blessed by Bilen, who altered the Kindos Sea to flow into the Ice Shelf.
