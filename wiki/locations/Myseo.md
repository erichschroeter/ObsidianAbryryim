# Myseo

Myseo is a diverse city known for its exceptionally hardy mounts and its unique communist governance.

## Description
Home to 23,000 souls, Myseo is the last major city before the lava plains. It is famous for its well-trained mounts, including griffins and elephants. The city is a communist state where all industry is controlled by the rules of Zultan Nor-Ten Four-Fingers, a hobgoblin expert at taming beasts.

## Interesting Facts
- Primary producer of mint, peas, cauliflower, and taro.
- Situated on Lake Yosef, the deepest freshwater lake in the world, which some believe has tunnels to the ocean.
- Lake Yosef is home to exotic creatures like giant squid and morkoth.
