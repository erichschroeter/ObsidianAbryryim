# Windmire

Windmire is a prosperous coastal city and center of arcane learning, home to the famed [[Alabaster Tower]].

## Description
Home to 22,000 souls, Windmire is the heart of maritime trade in the region. It features a magically carved deepwater harbor and is ruled by Highlord Kilhawn Wavefinger, Archmage of the [[Alabaster Tower]]. The city is known for its marble quarries and its massive shipyard.

## Interesting Facts
- Highlord Wavefinger trains [[Sea Wizards]] to aid navigation and naval warfare.
- The city has the largest shipyard in the world, building 3 large ships annually.
- Shimmering arcane beacons guide ships safely to the harbor.
- Highlord Wavefinger maintains a rivalry with General Korell Nugman of [[Thrumos]].
