# Kharkive Stronghold

Kharkive Stronghold is a former dwarven stronghold that was destroyed and occupied by Mindflayers.

## Description
Originally a dwarven stronghold in the first age, it was overrun by Mindflayers in the 2nd Age. 99% of the population was destroyed, and the remainder was enslaved. The city has six layers; the top two are in ruins and inhabited by mundane monsters, while the deeper levels are the domain of the Mindflayers.
