# Leyef Swamp

The Leyef Swamp is a vast bog or marshland characterized by high scrubs and patches of trees.

## Description
The water in the Leyef Swamp primarily comes from underground and is mostly undrinkable without purification. Some small villages exist within the area. It blends into the marshy shores of the Ancient's Pool near [[Elisheba]].
