# Knights Drift

Knights Drift is a major trade city and stronghold, originally founded by the [[Knights of Calor]].

## Description
Home to 50,000 souls (mostly humans and halflings), Knights Drift is built between two large lakes, Rechemay and Sybbyl. It is a major trade center and producer of wild rice, cranberries, and water chestnuts. The city is the base for the [[Knights of Calor]], now a respected mercenary company.

## Interesting Facts
- Home to "The Citadel," the oldest military school in Abryryim.
- The [[Knights of Calor]] honor the god Calor, who died during [[The Cleansing]] of the Age of Dawn.
