# Barony of the Key and Sword

The Barony of the Key and Sword is a prosperous region on the western frontier of the kingdom, known for its fertile valleys, trade roads, and strict adherence to law and duty.

## Heraldry
![[symbol_barony_of_the_key_and_sword.png]]
**Motto**: *"By the Key We Keep, By the Sword We Stand."*

The heraldic device features a silver sword (representing the oath to defend the realm) and a golden key (representing the trust of the crown and the authority to guard the western gate) crossed upon a field of green (representing the rich farmlands).

## History
The Barony was established after the [[War of Broken Crowns]]. The region had been plagued by brigands and monsters until [[Sir Aldric the Warden]] united the settlements. In recognition of his service, the King granted him stewardship of the valley to guard the western frontier.

## Governance
The Barony is ruled by [[House Aldric]], the descendants of Sir Aldric. It is a sanctuary for merchants, scholars, and priests, emphasizing that true strength comes from wielding power with wisdom.
