# Uur

Uur is a desert trade outpost primarily inhabited by orcs, bugbears, and other monstrous humanoids.

## Description
Home to 9,000 souls, Uur was once an oasis for nomads. It features few permanent buildings, with most structures being heavily decorated tents. The city has no ruler, and laws are enforced by the mob. It is a starting point for treasure hunters venturing into the Katoteros Desert.

## Interesting Facts
- Legend tells of the "Eye of Shem'lock," a ruby artifact that could command genies.
- The city is prepared to pack up and leave if a "Great Summer Sandstorm" occurs.
- Crafts include weaving, painting, and jewelry-making.
