# Snowshield

Snowshield is known as the coldest continuously inhabited place on the planet and houses the tomb of a dead god.

## Description
Home to 12,000 souls, Snowshield experiences extreme cold, with temperatures averaging -72°F. It is the site of the tomb of Nealdin, the god of thunder, who was killed by an ancient magical spear. Magic users study the constant thunder and lightning that rage in the skies above.

## Interesting Facts
- Nealdin's body is guarded by two thunder elementals.
- A magical dome protects the city from the extreme cold.
- Most inhabitants are reindeer herders or wild fishers.
- The fulfillment of a prophecy: "...the driving snow that bleeds will crown the next god of greed."
