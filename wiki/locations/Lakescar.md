# Lakescar

Lakescar is a city known for its hunters, bow makers, and woodworkers, as well as its strategic trade location.

## Description
Home to 13,000 souls, Lakescar is ruled by the halfling Lord Jasper Greenbarrow. The city is named after three lakes that resemble claw marks, formed by a glacier in the first age. It is a major trade hub for the south.

## Interesting Facts
- The lakes were formed by a glacier.
- Snow can reach depths of 6-8 feet during the winter months.
