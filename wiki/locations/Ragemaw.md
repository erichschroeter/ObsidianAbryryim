# Ragemaw

Ragemaw is a harsh city primarily inhabited by half-orcs and a "sulfic race," known for its violence and raid-based economy.

## Description
Ragemaw's population swells during "Muk-ta-Boya" (season of battle). The city has no formal ruler; power is held by strength. During battle season, tribe leaders take control and raid other cities for riches. The "sulfic people" are used as slaves and treated poorly.

## Interesting Facts
- The city's location between sulfuric fields and the desert makes its inhabitants exceptionally hardy, granting them an advantage on poison saves.
- The economy is entirely based on pillaging.
- Visitors can find items from across the land here.
