# Old Light

Old Light, formerly known as Blue Star, is a diverse island city known for its jewelry industry and its reputation as a pirate haven.

## Description
Home to 4,000 souls, Old Light was devastated by a sea dragon tsunami after inhabitants captured its child. Today, the city focuses on fishing, pearl harvesting, and jewelry. It is loosely governed by an elected council, but citizens largely live on an honor system. It is rumored to be a haven for pirates who hide in the Misty Moores.

## Interesting Facts
- The Alabaster Light House stands in disrepair as a reminder of former glory in sea dragon hunting.
- The Misty Moores are dangerous islands covered in heavy fog where cold and hot waters meet.
- The city is known for welcoming all races and cultures.
