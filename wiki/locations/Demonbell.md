# Demonbell

Formerly known as Wood's Bell, Demonbell is a city primarily inhabited by the undead [[Forbarrens]].

## Description
Demonbell is home to 13,000 souls, most of whom are undead. The city was renamed after the death of the god of life and death caused an explosion of essence that turned the inhabitants undead. Despite being undead, the inhabitants retain their personalities and lifestyles. The city generates wealth by selling detailed books, scrolls, and maps.

## Interesting Facts
- The ruler is a living human, as the [[Forbarrens]] see little value in ruling and the living take more pride in the city's appearance.
- The scary name was chosen to discourage people from moving in.
- New [[Forbarrens]] are made during every [[The Cleansing]].
