# Clayfield

Clayfield is the sister city to [[Bellfield]], primarily focused on the processing, refining, and storage of agricultural bounties.

## Description
Clayfield is home to 11,000 souls, mostly [[Avitum]]. While [[Bellfield]] handles harvesting, Clayfield manages processing and storage in large warehouses. It is a commune where work is volunteered, and there is no true governor. The buildings are built together, and citizens move freely through them.

## Interesting Facts
- Primarily inhabited by female [[Avitum]].
- Citizens are task-oriented and uninterested in global troubles or idle chit-chat.
- Most homes have doors but they are not locked.
