# Midkeep

Midkeep is a town founded on protection that has evolved into a consortium of traders and merchants.

## Description
Home to 10,000 souls, mostly halflings and gnomes, Midkeep was originally a fortified stronghold. Over time, the city's fortifications have fallen into disrepair, and the city has been shrinking. A river runs through the city, and a network of canals allows for small boat travel to most locations.

## Interesting Facts
- Catacombs beneath the city contain over 7,000 bodies and extend for 14 miles.
- Canals allow for navigation by boat throughout the city.
