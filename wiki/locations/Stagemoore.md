# Stagemoore

Stagemoore, the City of Industry, is a dynamic hub ruled by three powerful gnomish merchant houses.

## Description
Home to 17,000 souls, Stagemoore's population and focus shift constantly between Production, Service, and Transportation. The three gnomish families (ruling like a mafia) protect their own districts and goods. The city is known for its tinkers, artisans, and merchant lords who adapt to new booming industries.

## Interesting Facts
- Many tradesfolk live in traveling wagon houses due to the frequent changes in industry.
- The three gnomish council members frequently undermine each other for territory and power.
- The city can pivot its entire production focus (e.g., from bricks to armor) within a month.
