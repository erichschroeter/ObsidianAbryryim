# Marq

Marq is a logging community inhabited by wood elves who work in harmony with ancient Treants.

## Description
Home to 5,000 souls, Marq is known for its "Ironwood" – wood from trees infused with iron-rich waters, making it fire-resistant and extremely hard. The community follows a pact with ancient Treants to only harvest dead or dying trees. The town is run by a high council of elves.

## Interesting Facts
- The nearby Lake Aeris is home to fey creatures like faeries, pixies, and sprites.
- Dream Petals harvested here can be used to make an unconsciousness-inducing poison.
- Shadowtwig, a Treant envoy, facilitates communication between the elves and the Vathys Forest.
