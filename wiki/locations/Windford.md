# Windford

Windford is one of the wealthiest cities in the world, built into towering coastal cliffs and rich in precious gems and ores.

## Description
Home to 12,000 souls (mostly dwarves), Windford thrives on mining emeralds, mithral, pearls, and the legendary Dark Diamond Ore. The city features a vast network of flooded vaults for security, and is ruled by the cautious Lord Grizfane Goldentooth.

## Interesting Facts
- Home to the "Forge of Heaven," the only forge capable of shaping Dark Diamond Ore.
- The coast features 100 miles of clam farms, which are the main diet of the city.
- The city's vaults are among the hardest to breach in the world.
