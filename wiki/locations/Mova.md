# Mova

Mova is a logging city known for its high-quality timber processed through a unique soaking and blessing method.

## Description
Mova's population fluctuates from 15,000 during logging season to under 5,000 in winter. It is ruled by Lord Regent Vermillion St. Claire, an elven Druid. The city produces highly desired lumber, including "Iron Wood," which is soaked in bayous and blessed by Druids.

## Interesting Facts
- Vermillion commands the "Wooden Cavalry," an elite sect of horsemen.
- Iron Wood is sourced from groves where trees have absorbed high levels of iron from underground waters.
- Frigid winters cause the population to dwindle.
