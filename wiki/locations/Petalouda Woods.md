# Petalouda Woods

Petalouda Woods is a vast forest extending over 700 miles, known for its old-growth hardwoods.

## Description
The Petalouda Woods stretch from Etti Bay to the Notia Sea. The forest consists mainly of hardwoods like oaks and maples. Loggers from the region harvest trees from these woods to supply nearby towns.
