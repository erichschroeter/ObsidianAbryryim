# Silvermoore

Silvermoore is a city built largely within a mountain peak, famous for its silver and gold mining.

## Description
Home to 15,000 souls, Silvermoore is primarily inhabited by smiths, miners, and stone masons. About 50% of the population consists of dwarves and gnomes. The city is managed by six major mining companies. Only 10% of the city is visible from the outside.

## Interesting Facts
- Lava veins run through the city, providing heat and preventing snow on half the mountain.
- Home to a population of drow elves who moved underground during the first age [[The Cleansing]].
- A large pond at the base of the mountain peak contains hundreds of ancient trees trapped in ice.
