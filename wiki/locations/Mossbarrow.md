# Mossbarrow

Mossbarrow is a city whose livelihood depends on trapping crabs, sharks, and harvesting rare herbs from the Namqu Swamp.

## Description
Home to 17,000 souls, Mossbarrow is ruled by the Etpa Stone, a 40-foot-tall glowing black rock. Decisions for the city are made by making sacrifices to the stone; the more valuable the sacrifice, the better the answer.

## Interesting Facts
- The Etpa Stone is actually a prison for an angel trapped 2300 years ago; sacrifices provide life energy to maintain the prison.
- The city hunts "gulf sharks," which can reach 70 feet in length, for their fat and organs.
