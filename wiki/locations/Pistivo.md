# Pistivo

Pistivo is a city of religious acceptance, where temples dedicated to every deity strive to learn prophecies.

## Description
Home to 10,000 souls, Pistivo is characterized by its superstitious population and focus on divination. It is located near [[Iepos]], and its residents often search Pilgrims Bay for secrets from the island. The marshlands around the city are home to witches and hags.

## Interesting Facts
- Time-altering magics from [[Iepos]] have created a wildly diverse marine population in Pilgrim's Bay.
- Carnivorous sea grass and fish with irregular aging cycles are found in the nearby waters.
- Rituals, voodoo, and magical machinations are common in the surrounding marshes.
