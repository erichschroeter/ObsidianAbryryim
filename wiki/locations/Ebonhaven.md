# Ebonhaven

Ebonhaven is a thriving city nestled between mountains and a spring-fed lake, known for its unique religious government.

## Description
Home to 16,000 souls, Ebonhaven's economy is centered on mining silver, iron, and other ores. Despite being in a rain shadow, it has a reliable water source from a massive spring-fed lake. The city is governed by [[The Ruling Five]], a council of the leaders of the five largest churches, which are determined by an annual census.

## Interesting Facts
- The city is home to [[The Walkers]], adventurers seeking a safe trade route to Windmire.
- Religious competition is fierce as churches vie for membership to secure a seat on the council.
- The lake provides fresh water year-round in a dry and arid climate.
