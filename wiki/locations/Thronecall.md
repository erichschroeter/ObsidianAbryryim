# Thronecall

Thronecall is a harmonious and self-sufficient city ruled by an elven warlord who once slew a god.

## Description
Home to 9,000 souls (mostly elves and humans), Thronecall is isolationist and produces everything it needs. It is ruled by Lord Regent Barthos Batarie, a former warlord who survived a journey to the dragon lands.

## Interesting Facts
- Lord Barthos Batarie is one of the few to have slain a god; he carries the enchanted greatsword "Storm Cloud."
- Many people come to Thronecall to live out their final years in peace.
