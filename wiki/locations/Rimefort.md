# Rimefort

Rimefort is a city nestled in the "Land of Ten Thousand Hills," specializing in the mining and trade of salt.

## Description
Home to 11,000 souls (mostly dwarves and humans), Rimefort's economy revolves around hundreds of salt mines fed by the Ice Shelf. It is ruled by an appointed mayor and a master tradesman, each serving 5-year terms. The city is famous for its "Salting" season.

## Interesting Facts
- Two types of salt are mined: Black salt (high value, deep mines) and White salt (common).
- The land is said to "heave" with the annual ebb and flow of the local river.
- Former leaders receive a lifetime stipend based on citizen votes.
