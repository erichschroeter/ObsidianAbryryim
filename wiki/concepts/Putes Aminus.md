# Putes Aminus

Putes Aminus is a massive crater in Thrumos Bay, created when a shard of the moon Welun crashed into Abryryim.

## Details
The crater is known for disrupting magic in its vicinity, leading General Korell Nugman to ban arcane magic in [[Thrumos]]. It is rumored to be a portal to the two moons. Expeditions to the crater have been largely unsuccessful and deadly.
