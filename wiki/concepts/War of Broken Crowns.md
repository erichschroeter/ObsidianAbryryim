# War of Broken Crowns

The War of Broken Crowns was a period of significant conflict that predates the founding of the [[Barony of the Key and Sword]].

## Impact
- Led to tumultuous years of raids by rival lords, brigands, and monsters.
- Devastated fertile valleys and trade roads.
- Created the power vacuum and instability that [[Sir Aldric the Warden]] resolved by uniting the western settlements.
