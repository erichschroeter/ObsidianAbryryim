# The Cleansing

The Cleansing is a recurring historical event of immense significance that has shaped the history and population of Abryryim.

## Details
Details of the Cleansing vary by age. During the First Age, it involved major attacks on cities like [[Elisheba]]. It is often associated with the death of gods or major shifts in magical power. Some long-lived elves in [[Da'Hal Kempa]] have lived through the last Cleansing. It is a time of great upheaval where new [[Forbarrens]] are created and old civilizations are often tested or destroyed.
