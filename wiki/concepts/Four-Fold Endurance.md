# Four-Fold Endurance

The Four-Fold Endurance is a famous annual competition involving teams traveling to a series of locations throughout Quatois.

## Details
Teams of 3-5 compete while attuned to dimensional ankle bracelets that prevent teleportation. The event is a major source of profit for host cities like [[Iinshire]].
- **1st Leg**: Starts in [[Etti Bay]] (named after Captain Robert Etti, the first to complete it).
- **2nd Leg**: Starts in [[Fonius]].
- **3rd Leg**: Starts in [[Lakobos]].
The competition offers prizes including gold, magic items, and titles.
