# Forge of Heaven

The Forge of Heaven is a legendary forge located within the city of [[Windford]]. It is renowned for reaching extreme temperatures far beyond those of conventional forges.

## Significance
- **[[Dark Diamond Ore]]**: It is said to be the only forge hot enough to shape this legendary and rare mineral.

## Location
- Found within the coastal cliff city of [[Windford]].
