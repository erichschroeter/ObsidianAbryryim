# Aditus

The Aditus was a massive portal system attempted by wizards in the [[Katoteros Desert]] during the 2nd Age.

## Details
The portal was tied to a series of pillars within a 100-mile radius. The project ended in catastrophe when the wizards were infected with a magical plague and absorbed into their towers, which then exploded and created the desert.
