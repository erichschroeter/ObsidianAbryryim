# Dark Diamond Ore

Dark Diamond Ore is a legendary and extremely rare mineral, primarily found in the region of [[Windford]]. It is considered one of the most valuable resources in the world.

## Characteristics
- **Rarity**: Found nowhere else in such abundance as the hills of [[Windford]].
- **Durability/Hardness**: Implied to be incredibly resilient, requiring extreme temperatures to manipulate.

## Crafting and Processing
- **[[Forge of Heaven]]**: This is the only forge known to be hot enough to shape Dark Diamond Ore.

## Economic Impact
- The mining and trade of this ore (alongside emeralds and mithral) have made [[Windford]] one of the wealthiest cities in the world.
- It is stored in highly secure, flooded vaults beneath the city.
