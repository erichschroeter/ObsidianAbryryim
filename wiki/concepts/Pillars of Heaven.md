# Pillars of Heaven

The Pillars of Heaven are a series of massive stone pillars believed to be the resting places of dead gods.

## Details
Located near [[East Lake]] and [[Last Summit]], the pillars are a destination for crusaders and treasure hunters seeking holy relics. Artifacts are often encased within the pillars themselves, requiring dangerous climbs to retrieve them. The area is also known for deadly fumes and a resting place for artifacts like the Broken Crown of Kaikl.
