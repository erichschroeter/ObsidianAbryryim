# Sir Aldric the Warden

Sir Aldric the Warden was a legendary knight and the founder of the [[Barony of the Key and Sword]].

## Achievements
- **Unification**: United scattered settlements in the western valley during the raids following the [[War of Broken Crowns]].
- **Stewardship**: Granted guardianship of the western frontier by the King.
- **Founding**: Established [[House Aldric]] and the heraldry of the Crossed Key and Sword.

## Character
He was renowned for both his skill in battle and his wisdom in governance.
