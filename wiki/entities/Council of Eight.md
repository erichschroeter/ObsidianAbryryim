# Council of Eight

The Council of Eight is the governing body of [[Elisheba]], the oldest city in Abryryim.

## Details
The council promotes an ancient superstition regarding the power of the number eight. They strictly regulate the city's octagonal structure and its division into sectors and tiers. They make eight official visits per year to different sectors. The council holds a complete grip on the city's industry and maintains severe law enforcement.
