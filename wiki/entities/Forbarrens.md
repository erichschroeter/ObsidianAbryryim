# Forbarrens

The Forbarrens are an undead race primarily inhabiting the city of [[Demonbell]].

## Details
Most Forbarrens were created when the god of life and death died in [[Demonbell]], turning the inhabitants undead. Unlike typical undead, they retain their personalities, lifestyles, and memories. They do not crave blood and instead focus on learning and detailed craftsmanship. New Forbarrens are created during each [[The Cleansing]]. They are susceptible to the temporal effects of [[Iepos]], where they age to dust.
