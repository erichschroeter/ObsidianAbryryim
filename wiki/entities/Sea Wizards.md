# Sea Wizards

Sea Wizards are specialized spellcasters trained at the [[Alabaster Tower]] in [[Windmire]].

## Details
Trained by Highlord Wavefinger, these mages use arcane power to aid in navigation, manipulate winds and currents, and conduct magical warfare on the open seas. They also possess spells to reinforce vessels against fire and sea attacks.
