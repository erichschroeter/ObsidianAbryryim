# Alabaster Tower

The Alabaster Tower is a renowned center of arcane learning located in the city of [[Windmire]].

## Details
Ruled by Highlord Kilhawn Wavefinger, the tower is a hub for the study of magic and the training of [[Sea Wizards]]. It holds powerful spells used to reinforce ships and is a key influence in the region's magical affairs.
