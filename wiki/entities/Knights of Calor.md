# Knights of Calor

The Knights of Calor are a former religious order that has evolved into a highly respected mercenary company.

## Details
Originally founded as a stronghold for followers of the god Calor in the first age, the order now operates as a mercenary company based in [[Knights Drift]]. They provide protection along trade routes, serve as military advisors, and act as men-at-arms for various armies. They still honor the name of Calor, who died during [[The Cleansing]] of the Age of Dawn.
