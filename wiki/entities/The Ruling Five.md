# The Ruling Five

The Ruling Five is the unique governing council of the city of [[Ebonhaven]].

## Details
Political power is determined by religious influence. Each year, a census is conducted of the congregations of all recognized churches in the city. The five largest churches each earn a seat on the council for the following year. This leads to fierce competition among faiths for new followers and public influence.
