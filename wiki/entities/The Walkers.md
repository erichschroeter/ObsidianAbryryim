# The Walkers

The Walkers are a group of explorers and dreamers based in [[Ebonhaven]] dedicated to finding a safe trade route through the mountains.

## Details
For generations, the Walkers have searched for a secure path to the distant city of [[Windmire]]. While many trails exist, none are safe enough for regular caravans. Success would bring immense wealth and transform regional trade.
