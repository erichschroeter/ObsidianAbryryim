# House Aldric

House Aldric is the ruling noble family of the [[Barony of the Key and Sword]].

## Founders
- **[[Sir Aldric the Warden]]**: The first Baron and founder of the house.

## Philosophy
The house is known for holding law and duty above ambition. Their tradition teaches that true strength comes not from the sword alone, but from knowing when to sheath it.

## Motto
"By the Key We Keep, By the Sword We Stand."
