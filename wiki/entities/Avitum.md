# Avitum

The Avitum are a race of bird-like or nature-attuned beings who primarily inhabit the cities of [[Bellfield]] and [[Clayfield]].

## Details
The Avitum adhere to the traditional "Way of the Leaf." They are known for their agricultural skills and communal living. In [[Bellfield]], where most males live, they focus on harvesting. In [[Clayfield]], where most females live, they focus on processing and storage. They are generally task-oriented and have little interest in warfare or idle chit-chat.
