# Source: Barony of the Key and Sword

This document details the history and origins of the [[Barony of the Key and Sword]], founded by [[Sir Aldric the Warden]] after the [[War of Broken Crowns]].

## Key Takeaways
- **Origin**: Founded in the aftermath of the [[War of Broken Crowns]] to protect the western frontier.
- **Founder**: [[Sir Aldric the Warden]] (first of [[House Aldric]]).
- **Heraldry**: A silver sword crossed with a golden key on a field of green.
- **Motto**: "By the Key We Keep, By the Sword We Stand."
- **Governance**: Known for law, duty, and wisdom; currently ruled by the descendants of Sir Aldric.
