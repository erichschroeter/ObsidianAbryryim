# Cities of Abryryim

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the various cities, towns, and significant geographical locations across the continent of Abryryim. It details their populations, leadership, unique industries, and interesting historical or cultural facts.

## Key Regions and Locations
The source covers a wide array of environments, from the frigid [[Snowshield]] to the arid [[Katoteros Desert]], and the marshy [[Arachine Swamp]].

## Major Themes
- **The Cleansing**: A recurring historical event that has shaped many cities.
- **Diversity of Races**: Mention of Humans, Elves, Dwarves, Halflings, Gnomes, [[Avitum]], [[Forbarrens]], and more.
- **Industry and Trade**: Detailed descriptions of mining, logging, agriculture, and specialized crafts like gaza wine or ironwood.
- **Religious and Political Structures**: Unique governance models like [[The Ruling Five]] in [[Ebonhaven]] or the Council of Eight in [[Elisheba]].
